Sangwoo Heo 허상우

AI Safety Policy & Management Lead · Project Lead

NAVER Corporation, AI Safety Center  ·  Seoul, Korea

I lead AI safety policy and management at NAVER Corporation's AI Safety Center. Over the past two years, I have worked on establishing the company's AI Safety Policy & Governance Framework — contributing to risk assessment standards for model deployment and end-to-end safety red-teaming protocols for AI services, in alignment with Korea's AI Basic Act.

I am particularly interested in how new technologies change the way people work, learn, and allocate capital. I enjoy the early stage of a problem — when the shape of it is still unclear and there is no existing playbook to follow. Figuring out what needs to be built, and then building it, is what I find most rewarding.

Before moving into tech, I spent three years as a staff writer at Chosun Ilbo, covering government social policy — including Korea's early COVID-19 response and national pension reform. I later completed a master's in data science at Seoul National University, publishing first-author research on LLM hallucination detection. The combination — a journalist's instinct for framing, sensitivity to institutional context, and technical training — shapes how I approach new problems.
